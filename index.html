Our Story

From Nagoya to Penang

NIKUNO YOICHI is a beloved Yakiniku brand originating from Nagoya, Japan. We bring the authentic taste of Japanese BBQ to Gurney Drive, Penang, serving premium quality meats including Japanese A5 Halal Wagyu.

Our commitment to quality extends from carefully sourced ingredients to our signature sauces and the traditional iron pot rice cooking method that elevates every meal into an unforgettable experience.
